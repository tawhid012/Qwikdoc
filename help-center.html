Help Center

Find quick answers about appointments, account access, and platform usage with step-by-step support.

How do I book an appointment?

Search for a doctor by specialty or city, choose an available slot, and confirm your booking from the doctor profile page.

How can I manage my bookings?

Sign in to your patient account and open your dashboard to view, reschedule, or cancel upcoming appointments.

Provider account support

Doctors and clinics can use dedicated sign-in pages to access provider dashboards and manage availability.
